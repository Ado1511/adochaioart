Erdinger: A 360° Rebranding Campaign

Role: Art Direction, Strategy & Campaign Concept

The Foundation: Research & Identity

As part of my studies, I developed a complete 360° marketing campaign and rebranding for Erdinger. The first step was analyzing their market position to identify growth opportunities. I designed a more minimalist logo while preserving iconic elements, purposefully shifting the color palette to stand out from competitors.

The Concept: "You're Never Alone"

To create a resonant motto, I asked a simple question via survey: What comes to mind when you think of beer? The answers highlighted friendship, togetherness, and weekends. This birthed the core concept: "You're never alone with Erdinger"—a phrase emphasizing connection and camaraderie.

Offline Activation: The Berlin Bars

To introduce this fresh identity, we started in the real world. We sent Erdinger ambassadors to influential bars across Berlin with a simple mission: whenever they saw someone sitting alone, they would approach and buy them a bottle of Erdinger. Why? Because you're never alone with Erdinger.

Digital Expansion: Facebook & Instagram

As the new image gained traction, we moved online. On Facebook, we launched an interactive "dating app" feature that matched users based on their beer preferences, encouraging them to meet over an Erdinger. For Instagram, we designed highly interactive stories to encourage user-generated content, organically expanding our reach to non-followers.

The Result

By modernizing the visual identity and pairing it with creative, community-driven marketing, this project breathed new life into a well-established brand, successfully fostering a deep sense of connection with its audience.

Okami: The Asian-Italian Fusion

Role: Brand Identity, Art Direction & Conceptualization

The Brief

Create a compelling brand identity for an innovative restaurant concept that blends the delicate traditions of Asian cuisine with the bold, historic flavors of Italy, offering an unprecedented dining experience.

The Conceptual Bridge

How do you visually and conceptually bridge Tokyo and Rome? We found the answer in a single word: Okami (Japanese for "she-wolf"). This name creates a powerful, poetic link to the foundational myth of Rome—Romulus and Remus, who were raised by a she-wolf.

The Identity

The brand's visual centerpiece is a striking wolf logo that represents the strength of combining these two culinary empires. It sets the stage for a bold menu where cultures collide seamlessly—pairing sushi techniques with rich Italian ingredients, and elevating traditional pasta with exotic Asian flavor profiles.

The False Nine

Role: Founder, Art Direction & Apparel Design

The Brief

Develop a premium independent apparel brand that merges the disruptive, tactical strategy of football with unorthodox urban streetwear. The name refers to a player who breaks traditional formations, serving as the core philosophy for the brand's aesthetic.

The Brand Ecosystem

To bring this disruption to life, we created a rich visual ecosystem. The circular crest is a traditional badge of pride and heritage, while the logo is a clean, minimalist mark of modern identity.

This strategic distinction allows us to apply the brand creatively across high-quality garments, focusing on deliberate unboxing experiences and subtle, premium merch details.
